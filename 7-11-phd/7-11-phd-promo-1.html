How does PHD protect my privacy, especially with features that track my personal items?

Privacy is a core principle of Profile Hub Dossier (PHD). PHD is designed as a local-first app, keeping your information on your device whenever possible.

PHD does not access your camera, microphone, or location. It does not use your personal data for AI training, AI processing, or data harvesting. It does not upload your information to external servers or share it with third-party agencies.

Your data stays on your device, giving you control over your personal information. In short: keep privacy, stay local.
